The Best Movies According to Sai Krishna

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Jersey

A failed cricketer decides to revive his cricketing career in his late 30's despite everyone being skeptical of his ability to do so.

Nuvvu Naaku Nachchav

Venkateswarlu, aka Venky, comes Hyderabad to his father's friend's house where he meets an engaged girl . Things turn upside down when she expresses her love for him .

Lucky Baskhar

A cash-strapped cashier working at a bank embarks on a risky investment scheme and soon gets drawn into the murky world of money laundering.
